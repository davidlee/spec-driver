- **RegistryProtocol[T]** landed in `spec_driver/domain/registries/frontmatter.py` per ADR-009 §6.

  Registries declare `backlink_inputs` as data; zero relations imports in production registry code.

- **Shims**: `supekku/scripts/lib/{decisions,policies,standards}/registry.py` are clean re-export
  shims with canonical-location docstrings.
- **Creation**: Private `_create_governance_artifact` engine; 3 public wrappers preserved.
