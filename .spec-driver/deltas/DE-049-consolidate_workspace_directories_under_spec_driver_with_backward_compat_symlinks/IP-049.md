## 1. Summary

- **Delta**: DE-049 — Consolidate workspace directories under .spec-driver with backward-compat symlinks
- **Design Revision**: DR-049
- **Predecessor**: DE-048 (configurable paths via `[dirs]`)
- **Desired Outcome**: All content lives under `.spec-driver/`; backward-compat symlinks preserve old paths; all tests green

## 2. Context & Constraints

- **Current Behaviour**: Content scattered across `specify/`, `change/`, `backlog/`, `memory/` at repo root with intermediate grouping
- **Target Behaviour**: Flat layout under `.spec-driver/` per DR-049
- **Dependencies**: DE-048 (completed), ADR-004 (accepted)
- **Constraints**: No automated migration tooling; `kanban/` stays at root; `.contracts/` stays at root

| Phase                      | Objective                                                                   | Entrance Criteria | Exit Criteria                                                             | Phase Sheet          |
