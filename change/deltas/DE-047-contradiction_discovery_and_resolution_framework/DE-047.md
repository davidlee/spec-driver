## 1. Summary & Context

- **Backlog**: [ISSUE-039](../../backlog/issues/ISSUE-039-spec_corpus_reconciliation_establish_authoritative_prod_tech_specs/ISSUE-039.md) — Spec corpus reconciliation (umbrella)
- **Design Revision**: [DR-047](./DR-047.md)
- **Change Drivers**: Design intent has evolved; PROD specs contain contradictions and stale assumptions that prevent promotion to `active`

## 2. Motivation

The spec corpus cannot serve as an authoritative source of truth while
documents contradict each other on fundamental concepts. Agents and humans
encounter conflicting guidance, eroding trust and causing rework.

Before any PROD spec can be promoted to `active`, contradictions must be
surfaced, resolved, and the resolutions applied consistently.

## 3. Scope & Objectives

- **Primary Outcomes**:
  - Contradiction register documenting all cross-document conflicts
  - Design decisions codified as ADRs where architectural
  - All PROD specs updated to reflect resolved contradictions
- **Operational Constraints**: Human bottleneck on resolution decisions; batch related contradictions to minimise round-trips
- **Dependencies**: None — this is a prerequisite for all subsequent ISSUE-039 work

## 4. Out of Scope

- PROD spec promotion to `active` (that's Phase B / a subsequent delta)
- TECH spec creation or deletion (Phase C)
- Code changes — this delta is purely documentation/governance

## 5. Approach Overview

- **Corpus under review**: 16 PROD specs, 3 ADRs, 2 policies, CLAUDE.md, glossary, doctrine
- **Key Changes**:
  - Agent-driven cross-reference survey produces contradiction register
  - Human reviews and resolves each contradiction
  - Resolutions become ADRs (architectural) or spec revisions (local)
  - Updated specs checked for internal consistency

## 6. Verification Strategy

- **VA-047-001**: Agent survey covers all PROD specs and governance docs
- **VH-047-001**: Human reviews and resolves every register entry
- **VA-047-002**: Post-resolution consistency check — re-scan for remaining contradictions
- **Acceptance Criteria**: Contradiction register fully resolved; no contradictions remain between PROD specs and accepted ADRs

## 7. Risks & Mitigations

- **Risk**: Contradictions are deeper than expected, requiring significant design rework – *Likelihood*: medium – *Impact*: high – *Mitigation*: Triage by severity; resolve blocking contradictions first, defer cosmetic ones
- **Risk**: Resolution decisions cascade into large spec rewrites – *Likelihood*: medium – *Impact*: medium – *Mitigation*: Apply resolutions incrementally; one spec revision per resolved batch
